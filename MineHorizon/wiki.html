Welcome to the Minehorizon Wiki

Your comprehensive guide to the Minehorizon Minecraft Server.

Introduction

Welcome to Minehorizon, a Minecraft server dedicated to providing a fun and engaging experience for all players. This wiki is designed to help you get started, understand the server rules, and make the most of your time on Minehorizon. We strive to create a friendly and inclusive community where players can explore, build, and create together. You may suggest new features via Discord

Getting Started

  1. Joining the Server: To join Minehorizon, you'll need a Minecraft: Java Edition account (accepting "cracked" launchers) OR an Official Minecraft: Bedrock Edition account. The server address is: ares.5ijiusi.com
  2. Initial Setup: When you first join, you'll be in the spawn area. Take some time to explore and familiarize yourself with the surroundings. You may want to set your home using /sethome.
  3. Resource Gathering: To start building and crafting, you'll need resources. Head out into the world to gather wood, stone, and other materials.
  4. Building Your Base: Find a suitable location to build your base. Remember to hide it well to minimize greif.
  5. Exploring the World: Minehorizon features a vast and diverse world to explore. Discover new biomes, find hidden treasures, and embark on exciting adventures.

Server Rules

To ensure a positive experience for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:

  • Respect: Treat all players with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • No Griefing: Do not destroy or modify other players' builds or belongings without their permission.
  • No Cheating: The use of hacks, mods, or exploits that provide an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
  • No Advertising: Do not advertise other servers, websites, or products without permission from the staff.
  • Follow Staff Instructions: Listen to and follow the instructions of server staff members.
  • Use Common Sense: If you're unsure about something, ask a staff member. Use common sense and good judgment.

World Information

Minehorizon features a carefully crafted world with a variety of biomes, structures, and resources.

  • Overworld: The main world where you'll start your adventure.
  • Nether: A dangerous realm filled with valuable resources.
  • End: The home of the Ender Dragon, a challenging boss.

Plugins & Mods

Minehorizon utilizes a variety of plugins to enhance gameplay. Here are some of the key ones:

  • HuskHomes: Provides essential commands and features like /home, /spawn, and /warp.
  • LuckPerms: Manages player permissions and ranks.
  • WorldGuard: Protects the server's spawn from griefing and unauthorized building.
  • AuthMe: Protects players' accounts from account theft from malicious actors.

Economy

Information about the server economy

  • Ideology: We aim to give maximum freedom to our players, so there will be very frew regulations on playe-run markets
  • Player Shops: Player run shops are purely based on trust. If you get scammed, do not ask admins for a refund..
  • Currency: There is curently no offical currency. You are welcome to create one.

Farms

Information about creating farms & Farm policies

  • Types of Farms: There are currently no limitations on farm types.
  • Farm Efficiency: You may bring your farm's efficiency through the roof as long as it doesn't compromise the server's performance.
  • Redstone Farms: Redstone-powered farms are fine, but please limit farms to under 1000 redstone ticks per chunk.
  • Community Farms: It is up to you to maintain and operate your farm! Staff will not refund it if it is greifed.

Commands

A list of useful commands

  • /home: Teleports you to your home.
  • /spawn: Teleports you to spawn.
  • /warp [warpname]: Teleports you to a warp point.
  • /tpa [playername]: Requests to teleport to a player.
  • /sethome: Sets your home location.
