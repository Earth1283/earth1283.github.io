General Rules

Be respectful to all players and staff members
No harassment, hate speech, or inappropriate behavior
No cheating, hacking, or using unfair advantages
No griefing or destroying other players' builds
Keep chat family-friendly and appropriate

Building Guidelines

Respect other players' build space
No inappropriate or offensive builds
Report any issues to staff members
Don't claim more land than you need

Trading Rules

No scamming or unfair trades
Use designated trading areas
Report suspicious trading activity
Don't exploit game mechanics for unfair advantages
